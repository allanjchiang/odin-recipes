Tea Eggs

Juicy and healthy

This recipe will help you cook a delicious and nutritious meal.

Ingredients

  • 12 eggs
  • Star anise
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt
  • Coke
  • Tea leaves
  • Water

Steps

  1. Grab egg stove from under sink
  2. Wash eggs separately
  3. Wash eggs with pure water.
  4. Fill stove with water 'til almost full
  5. Pour out water, taking care not to pour out all the water or else the eggs will fall out.
  6. Fill stove with pure water until it goes over the eggs
  7. Add a dwell amount of salt.
  8. Twist stove around so the steam goes toward the kitchen
  9. Add 2 pink cups of pure water to outer Tatung electric stove or until the yellow line of yellow striped cup worth of pure water
  10. Cook eggs in electric stove
  11. Take eggs out
  12. Run with cold water, don't put cold water in electric stove
  13. Crack eggs with spoon
  14. Add soy sauce, half a can of coke, tea leaves and star anise into bowl
  15. Add water into outer stove and cook.
  16. Add 2 pink cups of water in outer stove and cook again if you want to marinate the tea eggs even more.
