Omelette

Best Vegetarian Omelette

Fills you up and energizes you!

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 0.25 red pepper, chopped
  • 0.25 cup Cheddar cheese, grated
  • a few leaves of fresh baby spinach
  • 2 cherry tomatoes, chopped
  • salt and pepper
  • 0.25 teaspoon butter

Steps

  1. Cut the cherry tomatoes, red pepper and spinach leaves.
  2. Melt the butter in the frying pan
  3. Beat the eggs
